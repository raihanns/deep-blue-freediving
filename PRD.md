# Product Requirement Document (PRD) - Dive Diary Academy

## Project Overview
Dive Diary Academy (DDA) is a premier freediving school based in Indonesia, providing AIDA International certified courses. The website serves as a landing page to attract new students, provide information about courses, introduce the instructors, and facilitate bookings via WhatsApp.

## Target Audience
- Individuals interested in learning freediving (beginners to advanced).
- Experienced divers looking for international certification.
- People seeking aquatic adventure and personal growth.

## Functional Requirements
- **Navigation**: Links to About Us, Our Instructor, Moments (Gallery), Courses, and Contact.
- **Hero Section**: Engaging background video, school name, brief description, and CTAs (Start Journey, Learn More).
- **About Us**: Information about the academy's philosophy, safety priority, professional training, and ocean awareness.
- **Instructor Profile**: Introduction to Jenifer, the founder and lead instructor, including her credentials and experience.
- **Courses Section**: Detailed list of available sessions:
  - Trial Session: Introduction to Freediving.
  - AIDA 2 (3 Days): Beginner Class.
  - AIDA 2 (4 Days): Extended Beginner Class (Recommended).
- **Pricing & Locations**: Clear pricing for each session and information on training locations (Aquatic GBK, Tribuana TDC, Puri Mansion).
- **Gallery (Moments)**: Visual showcase of freediving experiences with testimonials from students.
- **Contact/Footer**: Information on how to reach the academy via WhatsApp, Email, and Instagram.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Mobile Responsiveness**: The site must be fully functional and aesthetically pleasing on all devices.
- **Performance**: Fast page load times, optimized images and video.
- **Brand Identity**: Consistent use of brand colors (Ocean Dark, Coral, Gold, Sand) and typography.
- **User Experience**: Intuitive navigation and clear calls to action.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: React (Vite)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Icons**: Lucide React
